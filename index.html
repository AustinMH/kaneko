How Creative Are You?

Follow Japanese transplant to Omaha, and globally reknowned artist, Jun Kaneko around the world in "Kaneko's Monumental Risk.” He will explore the likes of San Francisco, Kyoto, Puerto Vallarta, Shanghai and New York in pursuit of his greatest and riskiest production to date. Kaneko will create three hours of projected animation on seven screens for the San Francisco Opera’s all new, six million dollar production of Mozart’s “Magic Flute.”

Will his wildly abstract fusion of traditional Japanese art and American modernism work in an 18th Century German Opera? Either way, Kaneko will inspire you to reflect on the nature of your own creativity.
